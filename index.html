Instructions:


Control Player1 with WAD keys to move left right and jump! Attack with spacebar

Control player 2 with you arrow keys to move left right and jump! Attack with your K key


The object of the game is to defeat your opponent before the timer runs out, if nobody wins before the timer runs out the player with the most hitpoints by the end will be the winner, if both players have the same hitpoints by the time the timer runs out it is a Tie!
